### About This Series

*Building Software on Immutable Facts* is an 8-part article series exploring a fact-centric approach to software architecture.

The series examines how immutable facts, rather than state, can serve as a durable foundation for modern operational, analytical, and AI-driven systems. It discusses themes including explainability, accessibility, portability, history preservation, and the role of facts in long-lived software systems.

The ideas presented are intended to encourage discussion and exploration of alternative architectural foundations for building and evolving software.
